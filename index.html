StorySpeaker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 21, 2025

Information We Collect

StorySpeaker only accesses the text that you manually highlight in your browser. This text is temporarily processed locally on your device to generate speech.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data (name, email, passwords, or payment info)
  • No browsing history, analytics, or tracking
  • No location data

Data Usage

Highlighted text is used only for text-to-speech conversion. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with anyone.
